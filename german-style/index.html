✦ Of This House

Reformed, classical & rooted in the shepherd's heritage.

We are a family of six in Deatsville, Alabama — homeschooling our four children through the great books, the catechism, and the daily adventure of life together. The name Shaeffer descends from the German Schäfer, meaning shepherd. We carry that name humbly, trusting the one true Shepherd who calls us by grace into a story far greater than our own.
